music

the first link is my bandcamp, where all of my released music is. the second link is the discography page on my personal website (katsumashi.com), where I go into more detail about the songs.

I am primarily a musician. the Katsumashi name actually refers to the music project, but I use it in so many places it's also my username. note: it is not my actual name.

the Katsumashi music project is a combination of a lot of different genres: bedroom pop & post-rock are the two big ones, but there are a lot of other influences in my music, namely indie rock, indie folk, progressive metal, & ambient, among others. the general idea of a lot of my music is this genre I made up called "post-pop" which centres around balancing typical pop melodies and song structure with the long, layered compositions of post-rock.

that sounds pretentious, but essentially it's just bedroom pop/rock but half the songs are 12 minutes long and 6 of those minutes are a big crescendo or whatever
